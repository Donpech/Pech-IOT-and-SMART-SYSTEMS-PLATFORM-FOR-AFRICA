## 2. SOURCE-BY-SOURCE SCRAPING & RESEARCH GUIDE

### 2.1 Google Maps — Business Discovery (Priority #1)

**Why:** Most comprehensive database of Nigerian businesses with addresses, phones, reviews.

**How to Search:**
1. Search by category + location: `"solar panel shop" Lagos`, `"pharmacy" Ikeja`, `"logistics company" Victoria Island`
2. Use Google Maps API (Places API) for automated extraction
3. Extract: business name, address, phone, website, rating, reviews count, opening hours, photos, categories

**Key Search Queries by Sector:**

| Sector | Search Terms |
|--------|-------------|
| Solar/Electrical | "solar energy company", "solar panel installer", "inverter shop", "battery store", "electrical contractor" |
| Healthcare | "hospital", "pharmacy", "clinic", "medical center", "diagnostic center", "dental clinic" |
| Education | "private school", "international school", "coding bootcamp", "training center", "university" |
| Logistics | "logistics company", "courier service", "shipping company", "warehouse", "clearing agent" |
| Commerce | "wholesale market", "computer accessories", "phone accessories", "electronics shop" |

**Locations to Prioritize (Lagos):**
- Computer Village, Ikeja
- Alaba International Market
- Trade Fair Complex
- Balogun Market, Lagos Island
- Victoria Island / Lekki (corporate businesses)
- Ikeja GRA / Maryland (mid-market)
- Apapa (logistics/shipping)

**Locations to Prioritize (Other States):**
- Abuja: Wuse, Garki, Central Business District
- Port Harcourt: Trans Amadi, GRA
- Kano: Sabon Gari, Bompai Industrial Area
- Ibadan: Dugbe, Challenge, Bodija
- Onitsha: Main Market, Bridgehead Market

### 2.2 Jumia Nigeria — Marketplace Sellers

**URL:** https://www.jumia.com.ng

**What to Extract:**
- Seller name / store name
- Seller rating and review count
- Product categories sold
- Price ranges
- "Sold by" information on product pages
- Response rate and ship-on-time rate

**Key Categories:**
- Electronics > Solar & Alternative Energy
- Computing > Networking Products
- Health & Beauty > Medical Supplies
- Phones & Tablets > Accessories
- Home & Office > Electrical Fittings

**How to Reach Sellers:**
- Many Jumia sellers list WhatsApp numbers in product descriptions
- Check seller profiles for alternative contact details
- Search seller company names on Google/LinkedIn for direct contacts

### 2.3 Jiji Nigeria — Classifieds Listings

**URL:** https://jiji.ng

**What to Extract:**
- Seller name, phone number (often displayed directly)
- Location (city, area)
- Product/service category
- Listing age and frequency (active sellers post regularly)
- Profile verification status

**Key Categories:**
- Services > Electrical & Computer Repair
- Electronics > Solar Energy
- Services > Logistics & Moving
- Services > Health & Beauty Services
- Vehicles > Commercial Vehicles (for logistics leads)

**Advantage:** Jiji often displays phone numbers directly, making contact easy.

### 2.4 Konga — E-commerce Sellers

**URL:** https://www.konga.com

**What to Extract:**
- Seller/merchant profiles
- Product categories
- Company names (look for business sellers)

### 2.5 LinkedIn — Professional Contacts (Priority #2)

**How to Search:**
1. Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator for advanced filters
2. Search by: job title + location + industry + company size
3. Extract: full name, title, company, location, connections, skills, endorsements, education, experience

**Key Search Filters:**

| Role | Search Terms | Location |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Solar Managers | "solar energy" OR "renewable energy" + "manager" OR "director" | Nigeria |
| Hospital Admins | "hospital administrator" OR "medical director" | Nigeria |
| School Owners | "school owner" OR "school director" OR "principal" | Lagos, Nigeria |
| Logistics Managers | "logistics manager" OR "fleet manager" OR "supply chain" | Nigeria |
| Digital Marketers | "digital marketing" OR "social media manager" | Nigeria |
| Graphic Designers | "graphic designer" OR "creative director" | Nigeria |
| Sales Professionals | "sales manager" OR "business development" | Nigeria |
| Pharmacists | "pharmacist" OR "pharmacy" | Nigeria |
| Doctors | "medical doctor" OR "physician" + "Nigeria" OR "Nigerian" | Global (for diaspora) |

**For Talent/Hires (Marketers):**
- Search: "field marketer" OR "brand ambassador" OR "sales representative" + Nigeria
- Filter by: years of experience, skills, current availability (look for "Open to Work" badge)
- Extract: full work history, certifications, education, skills

### 2.6 Instagram — Influencers & Business Accounts

**How to Search:**
1. Search hashtags: #NaijaInfluencer #NigerianBusiness #LagosLife #NaijaTech #SolarNigeria
2. Explore location tags: Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt
3. Use influencer discovery tools (HypeAuditor, Social Blade for metrics)

**What to Extract:**
- Handle, full name (from bio)
- Follower count, following count
- Average likes per post (engagement rate = avg likes / followers × 100)
- Bio link (often Linktree with all contacts)
- Email (often in bio)
- Content niche
- Location (from bio or posts)
- Previous brand partnerships (tagged posts with #ad, #sponsored, #partnership)

**Influencer Tiers:**
| Tier | Followers | Estimated Rate per Post (₦) |
|------|-----------|---------------------------|
| Nano | 1K-10K | ₦10,000-50,000 |
| Micro | 10K-50K | ₦50,000-200,000 |
| Mid | 50K-200K | ₦200,000-500,000 |
| Macro | 200K-1M | ₦500,000-2,000,000 |
| Mega | 1M+ | ₦2,000,000-10,000,000+ |

| Criteria | Weight | How to Verify |
