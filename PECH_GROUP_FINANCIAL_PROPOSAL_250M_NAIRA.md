## 1. Executive Summary

**PECH Group Holdings Ltd** is building **Africa's first vertically-integrated Commerce & Infrastructure Operating System** вҖ” a multi-platform ecosystem combining:

- **Embedded ERP** вҖ” Free business management software for African SMEs
- **IoT & Smart Systems Platform** вҖ” Hardware + cloud for energy, security, estate management
- **Smart Water Controllers** вҖ” Multi-tier water level controllers (Basic вҶ’ IoT вҶ’ Industrial/SCADA)
- **Smart Home & Electrical** вҖ” Switches, sockets, cameras (smart and non-smart), energy storage
- **Learning Tablets & EdTech** вҖ” Hardware for education content deployment with open API for creators
- **Yiwu-Style Marketplace** вҖ” Digital twin of Nigeria's physical wholesale markets
- **Spoke-and-Hub Logistics** вҖ” Cainiao-inspired parcel management platform
- **Payments Infrastructure** вҖ” PSSP/PTSP-licensed payment processing across all platforms
- **Advertising & LED Displays** вҖ” Digital signage and advertising infrastructure (Phase 2)
- **Self-Service Kiosks** вҖ” Multi-purpose payment and information kiosks

### Our Mission
PECH is a **technology and platform infrastructure enabler for people**. We build the digital backbone that African businesses, homes, and communities run on. Every product we ship вҖ” from a вӮҰ1,500 premium socket to a вӮҰ350,000 estate system вҖ” represents **reliability, quality, and good price for your money**. We sell high-end products AND equally good affordable products, but **never bad products**. Quality is our brand promise.

### Designed for Africa, Powered by PECH
All PECH products are **designed specifically for African conditions** вҖ” wide voltage tolerance (140вҖ“280V), heat resistance, dust/water protection, and offline capability. Every product is **progressively integrated across PECH's entire ecosystem**: the IoT platform powers device intelligence, the ERP connects business operations, the Marketplace enables discovery and sales, Logistics handles delivery, and Payments processes transactions. A PECH smart switch is not just a switch вҖ” it's a node in Africa's commerce and infrastructure operating system.

This plan details the deployment of **вӮҰ250,000,000** in secured capital across the first **18 months** of simultaneous lean platform launches, targeting **Nigeria first** with a clear path to West African expansion. Additional capital will be injected by the investor as the business gains traction.

## 2. The Opportunity

### Africa's Commerce Infrastructure Gap

| Problem | Scale |
|---------|-------|
| African SMEs without ERP/digital tools | 90%+ |
| Nigerian businesses using informal credit | 80%+ |
| African IoT market (2026) | $4.85 Billion |
| Nigeria food waste from cold chain failure | $2.3вҖ“3.3 Billion/year |
| Nigerian merchants on WhatsApp-only sales | 70%+ |
| Markets with zero digital presence | Thousands |

### Why Now

- Smartphone penetration accelerating across Nigeria
- Digital payments adoption (Paystack, Flutterwave, OPay growing rapidly)
- CBN pushing financial inclusion and PSSP licensing
- No dominant African commerce OS exists yet вҖ” **first-mover advantage is real**
- Nigeria's Data Protection Act (NDPA) favors local platforms

## 3. PECH Ecosystem Overview

![PECH Commerce OS Architecture](proposal_images/commerce_os_architecture.png)

PECH is modeled after **Alibaba Group's layered architecture**, adapted for African realities:

| PECH Layer | Alibaba Equivalent | Function |
|------------|-------------------|----------|

| Payments & Wallets | Alipay / Ant Group | Payment processing вҶ’ data visibility |
| Logistics Platform | Cainiao Network | Spoke-and-hub parcel management вҶ’ movement coordination |
| Fintech & Credit | Ant Financial / MYbank | Credit scoring from real data вҶ’ monetization |
| Data & APIs | Alibaba Cloud / Data Services | Trade intelligence вҶ’ sold to banks/governments |
| IoT & Smart Systems | Alibaba Cloud IoT | Energy, security, estate management вҶ’ infrastructure |
| Smart Hardware | Ant POS / Alibaba Devices | POS, meters, controllers вҶ’ hardware monetization |

> **ESCROW DURATION:** All CBN escrow deposits (PSSP, PTSP, MMO) are held **for the entire duration of the license** вҖ” as long as you operate under that license. CBN invests the escrow in treasury bills. Escrow is **refundable only when you surrender the license**. Annual license reviews assess performance targets.
>
> **COMBINED PSSP + PTSP CAPITAL:** If pursuing both licenses, total capital requirement is approximately **вӮҰ400M+** (вӮҰ200M PSSP + вӮҰ200M PTSP). A single entity may hold both with shared capital вҖ” requires CBN clarification.
>
> **MMO LICENSE (FOR WALLETS & ESCROW):** A PSSP license alone **does NOT permit** holding customer funds or issuing digital wallets. To hold merchant escrow, operate business wallets, or issue e-money, an **MMO license is required** (вӮҰ2B capital + вӮҰ2B escrow = вӮҰ4B total). Until the вӮҰ4B is justifiable, PECH will partner with an existing licensed MMO in a **white-label arrangement** to provide wallet services across all platforms.
