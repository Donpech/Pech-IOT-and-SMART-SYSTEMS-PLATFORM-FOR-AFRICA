## SECTION D: SCOPE OF GUARANTEE

1. **Financial Liability Cap:** The Guarantor's aggregate financial liability under this guarantee shall not exceed an amount equivalent to **six (6) months of the Employee's gross monthly salary** at the time the liability arises, unless a court of competent jurisdiction orders otherwise.

2. **Company Property Coverage:** The guarantee extends to the full replacement value of all Company property, equipment, documents, and assets entrusted to or accessible by the Employee during the course of employment.

3. **Duration of Guarantee:** This guarantee shall remain in full force and effect for the **entire duration of the Employee's employment** with PECH Group Holdings Ltd and shall continue for a further period of **twelve (12) months** following the date of termination of the Employee's employment, howsoever arising (whether by resignation, dismissal, redundancy, mutual agreement, or otherwise). This survival period is intended to cover any claims, losses, or liabilities discovered after the date of termination but arising from acts or omissions during the period of employment.

4. **Governing Law:** This guarantee shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, including but not limited to the provisions of the Nigerian Labour Act (Cap L1, Laws of the Federation of Nigeria, 2004), the Contract Law of the applicable State, and any other relevant legislation in force from time to time.

## SECTION E: GUARANTOR OBLIGATIONS

The Guarantor hereby acknowledges and agrees to the following continuing obligations:

1. **Change of Contact Information:** The Guarantor shall notify PECH Group Holdings Ltd in writing within **seven (7) days** of any change in residential address, telephone number, email address, or place of employment.

2. **Cooperation with Investigations:** The Guarantor shall cooperate promptly and fully with any investigation, audit, or inquiry conducted by PECH Group Holdings Ltd or any authorised law enforcement agency in relation to the Employee.

3. **Attendance at Meetings:** The Guarantor shall attend any meeting, hearing, disciplinary proceeding, or inquiry convened by PECH Group Holdings Ltd relating to the Employee, upon receipt of at least **forty-eight (48) hours** written or verbal notice.

4. **Production of the Employee:** In the event that the Employee absconds, abandons his/her duties, or becomes unreachable, the Guarantor shall use his/her best endeavours to locate and produce the Employee before PECH Group Holdings Ltd or any relevant authority within **fourteen (14) days** of receiving notice to do so.

5. **Reporting Obligations:** The Guarantor shall promptly report to PECH Group Holdings Ltd any information that comes to his/her knowledge regarding the Employee's involvement in any criminal activity, misconduct, or any act that may be detrimental to the interests of the Company.
