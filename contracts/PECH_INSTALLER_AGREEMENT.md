## 3. SCOPE OF WORK

3.1. The Installer shall provide Installation Services for PECH Devices, including:

   (a) **Site survey and assessment** — evaluating installation locations for suitability;

   (b) **Installation** — physical mounting, wiring, and connecting PECH Devices per technical specifications;

   (c) **Configuration** — setting up devices on the PECH Platform, connecting to networks, configuring parameters;

   (d) **Commissioning** — testing installed devices, verifying connectivity and data transmission;

   (e) **Customer training** — basic user training on device operation and the PECH dashboard/app;

   (f) **Basic maintenance** — routine inspections, firmware updates, minor repairs during the warranty period;

   (g) **Documentation** — completing Installation Completion Certificates, taking before/after photos, logging work in the PECH system.

---

## 4. CERTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

4.1. The Installer must:

   (a) Complete the **PECH Installer Training Program** (covering all device categories they will install);

   (b) Pass the **PECH Certification Examination** with a minimum score of 80%;

   (c) Maintain certification through **annual re-certification** (refresher training + exam);

   (d) Attend any mandatory training sessions for new device categories or platform updates.

4.2. PECH shall provide initial training at no cost to the Installer. Re-certification training costs shall be borne by: [ ] PECH [ ] Installer [ ] Shared equally.

4.3. The Installer's certification may be suspended or revoked for failure to maintain standards as set out in Clause 5.

---

## 5. QUALITY STANDARDS

5.1. All installations must comply with:

   (a) PECH technical specifications and installation guides;

   (b) Applicable Nigerian electrical and safety standards (SON, NEMSA);

   (c) Manufacturer's guidelines for third-party components;

   (d) Local building codes and regulations.

5.2. The Installer shall:

   (a) Use only PECH-approved tools, materials, and accessories;

   (b) Follow proper safety procedures at all times;

   (c) Leave the installation site clean and in good order;

   (d) Complete quality checklists for every Job.

5.3. PECH reserves the right to conduct random quality audits and inspections of completed installations.

---

## 6. EQUIPMENT AND MATERIALS

6.1. **PECH Devices:** PECH shall supply all devices, components, and specialized accessories at the agreed installer pricing. The Installer shall not source PECH Devices from unauthorized channels.

6.2. **Installer Tools:** The Installer shall provide their own tools and general installation equipment.

6.3. **Handling and Storage:** The Installer shall store PECH Devices in a clean, dry, secure location and handle them with appropriate care. The Installer is responsible for any damage to devices in their custody.

6.4. **Returns:** Unused or defective devices shall be returned to PECH within seven (7) business days.

## 8. WARRANTY OBLIGATIONS

8.1. The Installer **warrants their workmanship** for a period of twelve (12) months from the date of installation.

8.2. During the warranty period, the Installer shall rectify, at their own cost, any defects arising from:

   (a) Incorrect installation or configuration;

   (b) Use of incorrect materials or methods;

   (c) Failure to follow PECH technical specifications.

8.3. **PECH covers** manufacturing defects in PECH Devices under the manufacturer's warranty. The Installer shall assist with warranty claims by providing installation records and facilitating device replacement.

8.4. Warranty rectification must be completed within **five (5) business days** of notification, or within **twenty-four (24) hours** for critical system failures.

## 11. CONFIDENTIALITY

11.1. The Installer shall keep confidential:

   (a) Customer personal data and installation details;

   (b) PECH pricing structures and installer margins;

   (c) Technical specifications and proprietary installation methods;

   (d) Business strategies and partnership details;

   (e) Any information marked confidential.

11.2. Obligations survive termination for five (5) years (perpetual for trade secrets).

## 12. NON-COMPETITION

12.1. During the term of this Agreement and for twelve (12) months after termination, the Installer shall not install, distribute, or promote products that directly compete with PECH Devices in the same product categories within the Territory.

12.2. "Competing products" means IoT sensors, smart meters, solar monitoring systems, and networking hardware that perform substantially similar functions to PECH Devices.

12.3. This restriction does not prevent the Installer from providing general electrical or installation services unrelated to competing products.

## 15. TERMINATION

15.1. **By Either Party:** Thirty (30) days' written notice.

15.2. **Immediate Termination by PECH:** For:

   (a) Safety violations or negligence;

   (b) Quality failures affecting customer safety;

   (c) Fraud, theft, or dishonesty;

   (d) Failure to maintain certification or insurance;

   (e) Material breach of this Agreement (as defined in Clause 15.4);

   (f) Bringing PECH into disrepute.

15.3. **De-Certification Process:** Upon termination:

   (a) The Installer shall immediately cease representing themselves as a PECH Certified Installer;

   (b) All PECH branding materials, uniforms, and promotional items shall be returned;

   (c) All PECH Devices in the Installer's possession shall be returned or paid for;

   (d) Outstanding payments for verified completed Jobs shall be processed;

   (e) Customer handover shall be completed.

15.4. **Material Breach Defined:** For the purposes of this Agreement, "Material Breach" includes but is not limited to:

   (a) Failure to perform installation services for thirty (30) or more consecutive days without reasonable cause;

   (b) Violation of safety standards resulting in property damage or personal injury;

   (c) Unauthorized disclosure of Confidential Information;

   (d) Fraud, misrepresentation, or gross negligence in the performance of services;

   (e) Failure to maintain required insurance coverage or professional certifications;

   (f) Repeated failure to meet quality standards after written notice and reasonable opportunity to cure;

   (g) Unauthorized use of PECH intellectual property or branding outside the scope of this Agreement.

---

## 16. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

16.1. **Good Faith Negotiation:** Any dispute arising from or in connection with this Agreement shall first be resolved through good faith negotiation between the Parties within thirty (30) days of written notice of the dispute.

16.2. **Mediation:** If the dispute is not resolved through negotiation, the Parties shall attempt to resolve it through mediation administered by the Lagos Multi-Door Courthouse within thirty (30) days.

16.3. **Arbitration:** If mediation fails, the dispute shall be referred to and finally resolved by arbitration under the Arbitration and Mediation Act 2023, seated in Lagos, Nigeria. The arbitration shall be conducted by a sole arbitrator appointed by agreement of the Parties, or in default of agreement, by the President of the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA).

16.4. **Governing Law:** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

---

17.2. **No Agency:** The Installer has no authority to bind PECH or make commitments on its behalf.

17.3. **Entire Agreement, Severability, Amendment (written only), No Assignment without consent.**
