## 4. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

### 4.1 Commission Rates

The Marketer shall be entitled to Commission on Qualified Sales at the rates specified in the Commission Rate Table set out in **Schedule A** of this Agreement. The current base commission rates are as follows:

| Product/Service Category | Commission Rate (%) | Payment Trigger | Referral Period |
|---|---|---|---|
| IoT Devices & Hardware | 8% | Customer payment received | 12 months |
| Platform Subscriptions | 12% | First 12 months of subscription | 12 months |
| Marketplace Listings | 5% | Listing fee paid | 6 months |
| Solar Solutions | 10% | Installation payment received | 12 months |
| Logistics Referrals | 3% | Service fee paid | 6 months |
| Enterprise/Custom Solutions | Negotiable | As per SOW | As per SOW |

### 4.2 Volume Bonus Tiers

In addition to the base commission rates, the Marketer shall be entitled to Volume Bonus commissions based on cumulative monthly Gross Revenue generated from Qualified Sales, calculated as follows:

| Monthly Cumulative Sales (NGN) | Bonus Rate |
|---|---|
| NGN 0 - NGN 999,999 | Base rate (no bonus) |
| NGN 1,000,000 - NGN 4,999,999 | Base rate + 2% |
| NGN 5,000,000 - NGN 9,999,999 | Base rate + 4% |
| NGN 10,000,000 and above | Base rate + 6% |

### 4.3 Commission Calculation

**(a)** Commission shall be calculated on the Gross Revenue of each Qualified Sale attributable to the Marketer, as recorded and verified through the Company's CRM System.

**(b)** Volume Bonus tiers shall be assessed on the cumulative value of all Qualified Sales within a single calendar month. The applicable bonus rate shall apply retroactively to all sales within that month once the relevant threshold is achieved.

**(c)** For Platform Subscriptions, the Marketer shall receive the applicable commission rate on each monthly subscription payment made by the Customer during the first twelve (12) months of the subscription, provided the subscription remains active and payments are received.

**(d)** For Enterprise/Custom Solutions, the commission rate shall be negotiated on a case-by-case basis and documented in a separate Statement of Work (SOW), which shall be appended to this Agreement.

### 4.4 Commission Eligibility

**(a)** The Marketer must be the first to register a Lead in the Company's CRM System to be eligible for Commission on sales to that Lead. In the event of a dispute regarding Lead attribution, the Company's CRM records shall be considered the primary reference, and the Company's determination shall be final.

**(b)** Commission is earned only on Qualified Sales. No commission shall be payable on transactions that are cancelled, refunded, charged back, or otherwise reversed within the Clawback Period.

**(c)** The Referral Period commences on the date the Lead is first registered in the CRM System. Any purchases made by the Lead after the expiry of the applicable Referral Period shall not be eligible for Commission.

**(d)** The Marketer shall not be entitled to Commission on sales made to the Marketer themselves, their immediate family members, or entities in which the Marketer holds a controlling interest, unless expressly approved by the Company in writing.

## 5. PAYMENT TERMS

### 5.1 Payment Schedule

**(a)** Commission payments shall be made on a **monthly** basis. The Company shall calculate all commissions earned during a given calendar month and remit payment within **fifteen (15) business days** following the close of that calendar month.

**(b)** The minimum payout threshold is **NGN 10,000** (Ten Thousand Naira). Where the Marketer's accrued commission in any given month falls below this threshold, the commission shall be carried forward and aggregated with the following month's commission until the minimum threshold is met.

**(c)** Payments shall be made via bank transfer to the Marketer's designated bank account as provided in writing to the Company. The Marketer is responsible for ensuring that accurate banking details are on file with the Company at all times.

### 5.2 Withholding Tax

**(a)** All commission payments are subject to **Withholding Tax (WHT) at the rate of five percent (5%)** in accordance with the provisions of the Companies Income Tax Act (CITA), Personal Income Tax Act (PITA), and directives of the Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS), as applicable.

**(b)** The Company shall deduct WHT at source and remit same to the FIRS on behalf of the Marketer. The Company shall provide the Marketer with WHT credit notes for all deductions made, which the Marketer may use to offset their annual tax liability.

**(c)** The Marketer is solely responsible for the payment of all other taxes, levies, and statutory obligations arising from income earned under this Agreement, including but not limited to personal income tax, VAT (where applicable), and any state or local government levies.

### 5.3 Commission Statements

The Company shall provide the Marketer with a detailed monthly commission statement showing:

**(a)** All Qualified Sales attributed to the Marketer during the period;

**(b)** The applicable commission rate for each transaction;

**(c)** Any Volume Bonus adjustments;

**(d)** Any Clawback deductions;

**(e)** WHT deductions;

**(f)** The Net Commission payable.

### 5.4 Disputes

Any dispute regarding commission calculations must be raised by the Marketer in writing within **fourteen (14) calendar days** of receipt of the monthly commission statement. Failure to raise a dispute within this period shall constitute acceptance of the statement as accurate and final.

### 5.5 No Additional Compensation

The Marketer acknowledges and agrees that the Commission payable under this Agreement constitutes the **entire compensation** for services rendered. The Marketer shall not be entitled to any base salary, retainer, allowance, benefits, reimbursement of expenses, or any other form of compensation from the Company unless expressly agreed in a separate written instrument.

## 16. CLAWBACK

### 16.1 Clawback Period

Commission paid or payable to the Marketer shall be subject to clawback in the event that a Customer cancels, returns, or obtains a refund for a product or service within **thirty (30) calendar days** of the original purchase date (the "Clawback Period").

### 16.2 Clawback Process

**(a)** Where a Qualified Sale is reversed, cancelled, or refunded within the Clawback Period, the Company shall be entitled to deduct the full amount of Commission attributable to that transaction from the Marketer's subsequent commission payments.

**(b)** Where the Marketer's subsequent commission payments are insufficient to cover the clawback amount, the Company may invoice the Marketer for the outstanding balance, which shall be payable within **fourteen (14) calendar days** of receipt of such invoice.

**(c)** For Platform Subscriptions, if a Customer cancels their subscription within the Clawback Period, commission attributable to that subscription shall be clawed back and no further commission shall be payable on that subscription.

### 16.3 Chargebacks and Disputes

Where a customer payment is reversed due to a chargeback, payment dispute, or non-sufficient funds, the transaction shall be deemed not to have been a Qualified Sale, and any commission paid or payable thereon shall be subject to clawback under this Section.

### 16.4 Fraudulent Sales

Where the Company determines, in its reasonable discretion, that a sale was obtained through fraud, misrepresentation, or any improper means by the Marketer, the Company shall be entitled to claw back all commission paid on such sale, irrespective of the Clawback Period, and may terminate this Agreement immediately in accordance with Section 15.4.
