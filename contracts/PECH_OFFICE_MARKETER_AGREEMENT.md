## 10. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

10.1. The Employee shall be entitled to commission on all confirmed and completed sales attributable to the Employee, calculated as follows:

| Product/Service Category | Commission Rate | Basis of Calculation |
|--------------------------|----------------|---------------------|
| **IoT Devices & Hardware** | 5% | Of the total sale value (exclusive of VAT) |
| **Platform Subscriptions** | 8% | Of the first 12 months' subscription value |
| **Solar Solutions** | 6% | Of the total installation value (exclusive of VAT) |
| **Marketplace Listings** | 3% | Of the listing fees collected |
| **Enterprise/Custom Solutions** | As per approved SOW | Commission rate to be specified in each approved Statement of Work |

10.2. **Commission Eligibility:** Commission shall only be payable where:

   (a) The sale is directly attributable to the Employee's efforts, as verified by the Business Development Manager;

   (b) The customer has been invoiced and the Company has received full payment;

   (c) The sale has not been reversed, refunded, or cancelled within thirty (30) days of completion;

   (d) The Employee has properly recorded the sale in the Company's CRM system.

10.3. **Split Commission:** Where a sale is attributable to the efforts of more than one marketer or sales representative, commission shall be split as determined by the Business Development Manager, with the approval of the Head of Business Development. The decision of the Business Development Manager on commission allocation shall be final.

10.4. **Commission Cap:** Unless otherwise approved in writing by the Chief Executive Officer, the maximum commission payable to the Employee in any single calendar month shall not exceed one hundred percent (100%) of the Employee's monthly base salary.

10.5. **Commission During Probation:** During the probationary period, the Employee shall be entitled to commission at the rates specified in Clause 10.1, with no minimum threshold.

10.6. **Commission on Termination:** Upon termination of employment:

   (a) The Employee shall be entitled to commission on all sales completed and fully paid for prior to the termination date;

   (b) No commission shall be payable on sales completed or payments received after the termination date, regardless of the Employee's contribution to the sales pipeline;

   (c) Any commission paid on sales that are subsequently reversed or refunded shall be recoverable by the Employer.

10.7. **Commission Review:** The commission rates set out in this Clause may be reviewed and adjusted by the Employer at the beginning of each financial year, with at least thirty (30) days' written notice to the Employee.
