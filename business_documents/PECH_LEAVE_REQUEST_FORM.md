> **POLICY NOTES:**
> - Leave requests must be submitted at least seven (7) working days in advance for planned leave.
> - Sick leave exceeding two (2) consecutive days requires a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner.
> - Unpaid leave requests require approval from the Managing Director/CEO.
> - Leave may be denied or rescheduled based on operational requirements.
> - Employees must complete handover to their designated relief officer before proceeding on leave.
> - Failure to resume on the stated resumption date without prior approval may result in disciplinary action.
