## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. The maximum amount permissible under a single petty cash voucher is Fifty Thousand Naira (NGN 50,000.00). Requests exceeding this threshold must be submitted through the standard Payment Voucher process.
2. All petty cash disbursements must be supported by original receipts or other verifiable documentation. Expenditure without supporting receipts may be charged to the requesting employee.
3. The requesting employee must submit all receipts and return any unused balance within two (2) working days of receiving the funds.
4. Petty cash funds shall be used exclusively for the purpose stated in this voucher. Misappropriation of petty cash funds shall be treated as a disciplinary matter.
5. The Petty Cash Custodian shall maintain a log of all disbursements and reconcile the petty cash fund on a weekly basis.
6. The petty cash fund shall be replenished upon approval by the Finance Department when the balance falls below the minimum threshold.
7. This voucher is an internal financial control document of PECH Group Holdings Ltd and is subject to audit at any time.
