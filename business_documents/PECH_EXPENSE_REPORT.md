## TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. All expense claims must be submitted within fourteen (14) working days of the completion of the travel or business activity. Claims submitted after this period may be subject to delayed processing or rejection.
2. Expenses must be supported by original receipts. Where original receipts are unavailable, a signed declaration explaining the absence must be provided, and approval is at the discretion of the Line Manager and Finance Department.
3. Per diem allowances, where applicable, shall be in accordance with the prevailing PECH Group Holdings Ltd travel and expense policy.
4. Entertainment expenses must be accompanied by the names of attendees and the business purpose of the engagement.
5. Foreign currency expenses shall be converted to Nigerian Naira (NGN) at the prevailing Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) exchange rate on the date the expense was incurred.
6. The company reserves the right to audit any expense claim and request additional supporting documentation.
7. Any advance received that exceeds the total approved expenses must be refunded to the company within five (5) working days of submission of this expense report.
8. Fraudulent or inflated expense claims shall constitute gross misconduct and may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment, in addition to recovery of all improperly claimed amounts.
9. This document is subject to the internal policies of PECH Group Holdings Ltd and the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
