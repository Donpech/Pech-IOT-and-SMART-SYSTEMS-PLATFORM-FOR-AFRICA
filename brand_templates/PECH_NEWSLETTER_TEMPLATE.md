### Color-Coded Sections

| Section | Background | Header Color | Accent |
|---|---|---|---|
| Featured Story | White | Sky Blue badge | — |
| Two-Column Articles | Light Gray (#F5F5F5) | #1B2838 | Orange "Read More" |
| Team Spotlight | White | Dark Blue (#0099CC) | — |
| Quick Stats Sidebar | Sky Blue (10% opacity) | Sky Blue | — |
| Product Highlight | White | #1B2838 | Orange left bar |
| Footer | Dark (#1B2838) | White text | — |

---

### Content Guidelines

| Section | Word Count | Image |
|---|---|---|
| Featured Story | 150-250 words | 600 x 300 px hero image |
| Column Articles | 80-120 words each | 280 x 180 px thumbnail |
| Team Spotlight | 100-150 words | 100px circle portrait |
| Product Highlight | 80-100 words | 150 x 150 px product image |
| Quick Stats | 3-5 data points | Icons/numbers only |
