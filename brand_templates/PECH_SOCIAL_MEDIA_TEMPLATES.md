### Brand Voice Guidelines

| Attribute | Description |
|---|---|
| **Tone:** | Professional yet approachable, confident, optimistic |
| **Voice:** | Knowledgeable, reliable, innovative, community-focused |
| **Language:** | Clear, jargon-free (explain technical terms), inclusive |
| **Perspective:** | "We" for company posts, empowering language for Africa's tech future |
| **Avoid:** | Overly salesy language, excessive jargon, negative competitor mentions |

### Hashtag Strategy

**Always Include:** `#PECHGroup`

- Smart Systems: `#SmartSystems #SmartCity #Automation`
