### General Signage Rules

1. All signage must use approved brand colors only
2. Logo must never be smaller than minimum size specifications
3. External signage must comply with Lagos State Signage and Advertisement Agency (LASAA) regulations
4. All signs must be professionally fabricated — no hand-painted signs
5. Signage placement must be approved by Facilities Management and Marketing
6. Digital displays must use approved templates and rotate content on 10-15 second intervals
7. Damaged or faded signs must be replaced within 14 working days of reporting
