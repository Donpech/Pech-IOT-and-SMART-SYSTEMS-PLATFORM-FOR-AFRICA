## 8. GENERAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Approved Branding Methods

| Method | Best For |
|---|---|
| **Screen Print** | T-shirts, bags, large flat surfaces |
| **Embroidery** | Polo shirts, caps, premium items |
| **UV Print** | USB drives, hard surfaces, full-color small items |
| **Foil Stamping** | Notebooks, certificates, premium packaging |
| **Debossing** | Leather goods, notebooks, business card holders |
| **Vinyl Wrap** | Vehicles, signage, large surfaces |
| **Laser Engraving** | Metal pens, trophies, power banks |

### Quality Standards

1. All merchandise must use Pantone-matched brand colors
2. Logo reproduction must be from approved high-resolution files only
3. Samples must be approved before bulk production
4. All items must include "PECH Group Holdings Ltd" text (not just the logo)
5. Minimum order review by Marketing Department required
